What Can YOU Do?

1. ASK QUESTIONS
"What data trained this AI?"
"Who was in the training set?"
2. DEMAND TRANSPARENCY
Companies should disclose how their AI makes decisions.
3. DIVERSIFY TEAMS
Different perspectives catch different blind spots.
4. TEST ON EVERYONE
Benchmarks must represent ALL users, not just the majority.
Together, we can build AI that's fair for EVERYONE!
